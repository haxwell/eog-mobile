Welcome!

Easyah is a positive energy machine!

Our goal is to create more, and more, positivity in the world.

 To see how we'll do this, you first have to know about Do-er's and Encouragers.

 Do-er's

Do-er's are out doing things that people feel grateful for.

 Do-er's

When somebody wants to say Thank You to a Do-er, they can send that Do-er a Point.

 Encouragers

On the other hand, Encouragers appreciate what the Do-er's are doing, and want to help them in some way.

 Encouragers

In return for the Points that are sent to Do-er's, Encouragers make an offer to share their skills and resources.

 Rinse and repeat, and that's the basic idea behind Easyah!

0 To get started, ENCOURAGE someone by offering your special skill or resource to a fellow Do-er.
